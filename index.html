Havona is a progressive  metal band based in Saint Louis, MO. It was
founded in 2016. At that time, the band consisted of the bassist and
then-drummer. The original idea of this band was
a mix of progressive metal and groove metal, with influences
such as the ones listed below.

Influences

Influences in this band, all pooled in from current and some former members, include, but not limited to:

  • Lamb of God
  • Iron Maiden
  • Machine Head
We are currently searching for a drummer. You must be
18+ years of age, have reliable transportation, your own equipment,
can not have any chronic alcohol or drug additction, can not have a
felony on your criminal record, and you can not be currently serving
a probation or parole sentence.
We don't neeed a vocalist, but if you want to try out for
vocals, same standards apply to you. And also, while we do not consider
ourselves to be virtusosos, this band is an open an honest environment,
so you are going to have to be receptive to constructive criticism,
even  when you might be tempted not to.
